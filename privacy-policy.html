Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026  ·  We believe in transparency about how your data is handled.

1. Introduction

  • Welcome to Nkommo, a product of Blektech ("we", "our", or "us") — a community forum app for immigrants and curious minds. By using our app, you agree to this Privacy Policy, which explains how we collect, use, and share your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address or authentication data from Google/Apple, plus any optional username you provide.
  • User Content: Any text posts or comments you submit, which may be publicly visible to other users.
  • Firebase: We use Firebase for database and authentication services, which may involve collecting data as described above.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Providing the Service: Authenticating your account and displaying your posts and comments.
  • Security: Monitoring for unauthorized access and ensuring the app's integrity.
  • Improvements: Analysing usage trends (e.g., crash reports) to enhance features, performance, and user experience.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • Service Providers: We share data with Firebase and other trusted partners who help operate our app. They only access your data to perform tasks on our behalf.
  • Authentication Providers: If you sign in with Google or Apple, we share relevant data with them to verify your identity.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose data if required by law or to protect our rights and the safety of others.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Cloud Servers: We store data on secure servers with industry-standard protection.
  • Retention: We may retain data for as long as your account remains active. You can request deletion of your account and data at any time, subject to any legal obligations.
  • Breach Response: In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and comply with applicable laws.

6. Your Rights

  • Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, update, or delete your personal data. To make a request, email us at support@blektech.com.

7. Children's Privacy

  • Nkommo is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will post the updated policy on this page and update the effective date. Your continued use of Nkommo indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
